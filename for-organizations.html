    Common questions

    Is Serenio a replacement for therapy?

    No. Serenio is a mental-health companion, not a medical provider.

    Who can use Serenio?

    The web app service is intended for people aged 18 and over.

    Are my conversations private?

    Yes. We don't share your conversations unless you agree.

    What languages does Serenio speak?

    English, Traditional Chinese, Japanese and Korean.

    How much does it cost?

    Serenio is free for everyone, on LINE and in the web app.

    Can we bring Serenio to our organization?

    Yes. Reach out and we'll find the right way to bring Serenio to your people.
